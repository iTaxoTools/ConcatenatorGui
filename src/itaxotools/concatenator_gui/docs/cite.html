Concatenator was developed by V. Kharchev and S. Patmanidis in the framework of the iTaxoTools project:
Vences, M., A. Miralles, S. Brouillet, J. Ducasse, A. Fedosov, V. Kharchev, I. Kostadinov,
S. Kumari, S. Patmanidis, M.D. Scherz, N. Puillandre, S.S. Renner (2021):
iTaxoTools 0.1: Kickstarting a specimen-based software toolkit for taxonomists. - Megataxa 6: 77-92.

Concatenator integrates Mafft (by K. Katoh and collaborators), FastTree (by M. N. Price),
SequenceBouncer (by C. D. Dunn), Gblocks (by J. Castresana) and ClipKIT (by J. L. Steenwyk).
